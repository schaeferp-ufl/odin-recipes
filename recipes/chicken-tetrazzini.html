Chicken Tetrazzini

Description

A cheesy creamy italian noodle dish served with or without chicken as well as any vegetables you desire. Takes roughly an hour to cook.

Ingredients

  • Shredded Chicken
  • 1 package wide egg noodles
  • 2 cans Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 2 cans Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 16 oz Sour Cream
  • 1 Large Container Shredded Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 cup Panko Breadcrumbs

Steps

  1. Cooking the Chicken

    1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and thyme.
    2. Add olive oil to skillet heated at medium-low temperature.
    3. Place chicken into pan and cover for 10 minutes. Check occasionally.
    4. Flip chicken after 10 minutes or if side is desireably brown.
    5. Remove chicken from heat once internal temperature reaches 165.
    6. Shred with two forks and set to the side.

  2. Cooking the Noodles

    1. Cook according to the package.
    2. Thats it :)

  3. Preparing the Mixture

    1. In a large bowl, mix soup and sour cream.
    2. Add in parmesan a little at a time until homogeneously mixed.
    3. Add in noodles and mix well.
    4. Add in chicken and mix well.

  4. Baking

    1. Preheat oven to 350.
    2. Put mixture into a large glass baking dish and evenly distribute.
    3. Mix one part parmesan cheese and one part panko breadcrumbs and top mixture
    4. Place baking dish into oven and cook for 30-35 minutes or until top is golden brown and mixture is bubbling.
