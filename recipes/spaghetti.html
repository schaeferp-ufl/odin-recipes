Spaghetti

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 jars tomato sauce
  • 2 large white onions
  • 2 large red bell peppers
  • 16 oz baby bella mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1-2 pounds ground beef
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Minced garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Thyme
  • Desired type and amount of noodles (spaghetti, angel hair, etc)

Steps

  1. Preparation

    1. Dice onions into small cubes
    2. Chop red bell peppers into thin strips
    3. Season ground beef with salt, pepper, thyme, and garlic powder

  2. Making the Sauce

    1. Heat olive oil and butter in a sauce pan on medium-high heat
    2. Add diced onions in a cook for 3 minutes or until translucent
    3. Add in two tablespoons of minced garlic and cook for 45 seconds
    4. Add in 16 oz baby bella mushrooms
    5. Add in two tablespoons of soy sauce
    6. Cook for 8 minutes or until mushrooms are tender
    7. Add in ground beef, mix thoroughly, and cook for until ground beef is browned throughout
    8. Add in both jars of tomato sauce
    9. Bring to a boil then reduce heat to low and simmer until serving

  3. Cooking the Noodles

    1. Fill a medium pot with 2 quarts of water
    2. Generously salt water to prevent sticking and add flavor
    3. Add 1 tablespoon of olive oil to water
    4. Bring water to a vigorous boil
    5. Add noodles and cook to package specifications

  4. Serving

    1. Add as many noodles as desired to whichever dish you desire (bowl, plate, etc.)
    2. Add a liberal amount of sauce to the noodles
    3. Top with desired amount of parmesan cheese
